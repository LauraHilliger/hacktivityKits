Color Terminology

  • Hue

    The color part of a color, such as blue.

  • Saturation

    The intensity of that color.

  • Brightness

    The amount of lightness or darkness

  • Neutral

    Black, white, or gray; also known as nonchromatic hues.

  • Chromatic

    Not black, white or gray.

  • Monochromatic

    Color combinations using tints and shades of a single hue.

  • Warm vs Cool

    Red, orange, yellow are "warm" colors. Blue, indigo and violet are "cool" colors.
