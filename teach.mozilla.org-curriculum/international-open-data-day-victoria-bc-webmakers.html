Open Data Day - Victoria BC:

Webmaker Hacktivity

Memories of our City.

Dig into your subconscious and find memories that are locally based in Victoria BC. Then, make an interactive project to help everyone understand why Victoria is so awesome.

Steps

  1. Remember when? In small groups share some of your favourite memories as they relate to locations in Victoria.
  2. Your Memory as a Story

    Using your Notebook & pen write a list:

    • Briefly describe your best memories, and where they took place.
    • Write down what makes those memories stand out. Was it the sunny day, or the wicked scar you got on your knee as a result?
    • How old were you at the time?
  3. Plan your story

    • Does your memory list have a theme? Could it be summarized with a song or video? Try to find a common theme in your memories - even if it's something as simple as 'having fun', or 'breaking the rules'.

  4. Tell your Story using Popcorn Maker
  5. Select a song, video (or make your own recording) that best reflects your memory theme. Use Popcorn Maker to tell the story of your memories using your selected media. If you need help getting started with Popcorn Maker, try the tutorial or remix the Neighborhood Tour Popcorn project.
  7. Plot your Story in the Victoria Wiki
  8. Once you're done your Popcorn Project, grab the embed code and add it to the Victoria Wiki.

Materials

  • Computer
  • Web Access
  • Notebook & Pen
  • Cameras (optional)
  • Popcorn Maker Login Credentials
