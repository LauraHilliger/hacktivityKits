Innovative Community Building

These initiatives used creativity and out-of-the-box thinking to jumpstart community growth in local areas or in the digital space.

What do these initiatives have in common?

  • They represent online and offline communities
  • They connect to people in an effort to promote web literacy.
  • They're centered around a maker approach.

How do they work?

These initiatives use the Web as their platform to stay connected when collaborators are not in the same physical space. The meta-designers behind these initiatives use common social networking and communication tools to keep their projects humming, and they develop with an open source mindset.
