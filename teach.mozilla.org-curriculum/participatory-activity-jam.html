Activity

Participatory Activity Jam

Create two teams and have them come up with a participatory activity that introduces a topic or technical skill.

Made by Laura

Steps for the Activity

  1. Dive the group into two teams. Ask participants not to talk until you say GO!

  2. Give the groups 20 minutes to come up with a group activity that introduces a web literacy competency or technical skill or one which makes discussion interactive and fun. Do not allow them to write down or document their activities. Tell them there are bonus points for:

    • complexity
    • using humor
    • facilitating an activity that includes documentation
    • activities that require everyone to move around the room
    • activities that introduce difficult concepts

  3. Have Group 1 lead their activity, and encourage Group 2 to give feedback.

  4. Now have Group 2 lead while Group 1 gives feedback.

Materials

  • Markers
  • Paper

Discussion

At the end, ask each team how they felt about the activities, time constraints and "competition".
