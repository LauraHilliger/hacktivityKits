Activity

Speed Geek Connecting

This activity creates and open space to play with different projects in a speedy format. This initial connected learning experience helps people get their feet wet with these kinds of pedagogies.

Made by Laura and Sam

Steps for the Activity

  1. Set up a table for 6 people who have a hands-on project to show. Divide the rest of the group into subgroups. There should be one group for each station

  2. Tell hosts they will have 5 minutes to both give participants a challenge or task and have participants complete it. Sample projects might be quick remixes, drawings or even a small craft activity.

  3. Use a timer to rotate groups around the different stations the room.

Materials

  • Pens
  • Post-its
  • Computer
  • Web Access

Discussion

How did it feel to be creative in five minute increments? What about this activity made you unfcomfortable? What did you like? How does making influence your learning?
