Teaching Kit

Telefonica + Mozilla Training

This kit documents the agenda we'll use at the Telefonica + Webmaker Training event November 3rd and 4th in London. Participants are encouraged to complete some preparation work before we meet.

Made by Laura

Description

During the first day of training we will collectively document what we want to learn and dive into pedagogies and toolsets that can support our work. We'll use participatory methods to critically examine our individual situations while developing resources and methods to improve how we train Think Big program managers and mentors. Our aim is to help them apply open practices and participatory pedagogies into their programs. We will learn about openness and collaboration by being open and collaborative. Then we will reflect on what we've accomplished in a single day.

On the second day of training, we'll further strengthen our understanding of collaborative works and repurposing previously existing methods and content. We'll work with Mozilla's Open Education Resource model, and start thinking about how we can apply what we've learned in various Telefonica situations. Then we'll reflect (through making) on the training and determine where we go from here.

Assessment and review

  • Discussion questions: How will we take our learning forward? What skills will we embed in our programs? What does it mean to run a participatory program? Why is it advisable to focus on the learner's interests? Why is it ok to fail? How can you get in touch with the Mozilla community? Where should you go for help?
  • Peer Assessment: Look at one another's work and give each other constructive feedback. Hold each other accountable for participation, and encourage open discussion with one another.
  • Sharing. If you use twitter, use the #TeachTheWeb hashtag to share what you're making and doing. Otherwise, post and respond in Discourse.

What you'll make together

Over the course of two days, we'll make mindmaps, paper prototypes, teaching kits, apps and more.

Learning objectives

At the end of the workshop, we will understand how to:

  • recognize opportunities to strengthen bridges between Telefonica / Think Big and Mozilla.
  • work in the open, and we will know why it's important in our contexts
  • facilitate participatory workshops and use learner-centric design
  • collaborate in an online environment and facilitate offline collaboration
  • build curriculum with the intention of sharing and remix
  • remix content that aligns with the Web Literacy Map
  • use the Webmaker tools, including Appmaker
  • accept one another as a valuable ally and feel confident in reaching out to ask for or provide feedback
  • stay connected and accountable to one another
