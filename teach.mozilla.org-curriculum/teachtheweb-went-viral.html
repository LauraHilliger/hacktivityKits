Pedagogically Revolutionary! Edsurge Review The gap between technology and education is virtually non-existent because of the #teachtheweb team. You have to read how they did it. Sir Tim Berners Lee

In 2014 the #teachtheweb team at Mozilla set out to design and initiate a comprehensive training program that could be easily remixed and reproduced and content to support any one who wanted to teach the web. They planned to run sprints to make content that aligned with the Web Literacy Standard and worked to co-design resources and agendas with communities worldwide.

Their work inspired a swift moving educational and technological revolution. From Bratislava to Bangkok, schools, museums, universities and other cultural institutions are running #teachtheweb to level up skills of their employees and constituents.

In this brilliant reflection, the #teachtheweb Team talks about the trials and tribulations that inevitably led to the time of Teach the Web. They show us how working in community can change the world, and how their often hilarious journey led them to entirely new pedagogical and technological models.
