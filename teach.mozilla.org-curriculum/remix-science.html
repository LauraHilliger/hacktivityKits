Activity

Remixing Science

In this activity, learners remix a Popcorn video about Earth's Atmosphere to be about Earth's Unique qualities compared to other planets in our Solar System.

Made by Laura Hilliger

Steps for the Activity

  1. We'll be using this popcorn project. Change the first text element in Popcorn to explain why Earth is unique. What does our planet have that other planets do not?

    • Atmospherically: Our Atmosphere has some unique featues. Name one.
    • Structurally: The...
    • Etc: Well-planned so that concrete outcomes are achieved in the allotted time, and participants feel time was well spent.

  2. How is our weather unique?

  3. Be descriptive! Seeing an activity in action is very different than reading about it, so be as specific as you can.

Materials

  • Pens
  • Post-its
  • Computer
  • Web Access
