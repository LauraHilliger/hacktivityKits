Activity

Remix-a-thon: Brainstorm Your Ideal Action

Introduce learners to an organic, interactive design process for webmaking. Participants play with Thimble and the X-Ray Goggles to brainstorm activity in a local area.

Made by Laura

Steps for the Activity

  1. Explore what you wish to acheive by teaching in your community:

    • Who in your community do you care about teaching? Why?
    • What skills or knowledge do you want to share with them?
    • How would this knowledge benefit them?
    • How can you share this experience with your peers?

  2. Play with Thimble and the X-Ray Goggles to document initial thoughts: Have learners work in pairs to remix this page to include their own initial thoughts on social actions they wish to lead in their communities.

  3. Set a date, and enter your event into the Webmaker Events platform. You can always change the date later, and setting one will give you a goal to work towards.

Materials

  • Computer
  • Web Access
  • A blog or Etherpad for posting work and/or links

Discussion

Have participants talk about their event ideas and display the work they have done.

Following the presentations, facilitators should direct participants to other resources in their community or online to learn more about hacking and web design/development. Provide individual constructive feedback during presentations.
