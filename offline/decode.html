Decode the Code

Offline Hacktivity

What this page is

Look closely at the following HTML elements and try to imagine what they should look like. Pretend you are a computer and your job is to translate the HTML into words and images. Draw, write, color and interpret the code. Compare it with other people and see which ones are correct. Then make up your own!
