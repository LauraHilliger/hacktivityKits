Young Rewired State NYC - 2013

All Badges

  • Peer to Peer

    Awarded to every young person who helps someone else with their work during YRS NYC 2013.

  • Coder

    Awarded to every young person that writes code during YRS NYC 2013.

  • Designer

    Awarded to every young person who creates a compelling graphic or design during YRS NYC 2013.

  • Polycoder

    Awarded to every young person who writes code in more than one language during YRS NYC 2013.

  • Presenter

    Awarded to every young person who presents their hack or design at the YRS NYC 2013 presentation.

  • YRS 2013 Alumnus

    Awarded to every young person that participates in YRS NYC 2013.

  • Code a Better City

    The hack the judges consider the most useful for civic engagement.

  • Best Example of Coding

    The best constructed code in hack.

  • Best Example of Design

    The most well-designed interface or prototype in hack.

  • I Wish I'd Thought of That

    The concept for a hack that most impressed the judges for its creativity.

  • "Should Exist" Award

    The concept for a hack that most impressed the judges by its innovation.

  • SAP Best in Show Award

    The concept for a hack that most impressed the judges by its design, originality and coding.
