How to Hack:

a Boardgame

Icebreaker

Participants change the rules of a popular boardgame to hack the game play.

Back to the overview View other kits

Steps

  1. Everyone breaks into groups of no more than five people and finds a space to work in.

  2. Facilitators explain that we will be changing the rules of the game to form a new game.

  3. Groups work to redefine the rules of the board game either by playing the game and coming up with new rules as they go, or by creating a new set of rules before starting to play.

  4. Collect the game rules that participants come up and post them on the wall.

Materials

  • Speakers
  • Computer
  • Web Access
  • Projector
  • Poster Paper
  • Markers
  • A few boardgames. Monopoly works really well, but any boardgame can be hacked.
